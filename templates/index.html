HandFont v1.0

Custom Handwriting Fonts

Turn your
handwriting into
a real font

Print a template, fill in every character by hand, scan it back, and download a complete font family in TTF, OTF, and WOFF2.

Step 01

Generate your template

Choose your character sets and download a print-ready PDF. Works perfectly opened on iPad for Apple Pencil input.

Step 02

Fill in every character

Write each character in its cell. Use the blue baseline as a guide. Keep size and slant consistent throughout.

Step 03

Upload & get your font

Upload the completed pages. The app extracts each glyph, vectorises it, and packages a full font set you can install.

iPad tip: Open the PDF in the Files app, then use Markup or any drawing app with a layer on top. When done, export as a flat image or PDF and upload it in the Build step. The app handles the rest automatically.
