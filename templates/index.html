Getting Started

Guidelines for a perfect font

  • 1
    Choose your medium

    Download the PDF template. You can print it to write by hand, or import it into a tablet to draw the characters digitally.

  • 2
    Ensure high legibility

    Avoid fully closed loops (like in 'A', 'P' or 'o'). Leaving a microscopic gap ensures the software traces the ink perfectly.

  • 3
    Proportions & Boundaries

    Stay within the lines and maintain natural proportions. Don't worry about being perfect—you can fine-tune every character's size and position in the Studio later.
